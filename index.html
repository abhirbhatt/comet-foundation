    • About Educenter Hyderabad

    COMET Study Centre (CSC) is like a PG accomodation that provides food and accomodation to all students. CSC helps class 12 pass out students in following ways –

    • Help students with admission procedure in Osmania university or any other university/college as per students’ choice.

    • Provides secure and very effective study environment.

    • Provides FREE sessions to all students on advanced tools and technology, and IT job profiles oriented training.

    • Weekly workshops and internal doubt clearing sessions.

    • Sessions relaed to group dicussion, soft-skills, englisht communication, and mock-interviews.

    • 24X7 supervision and guidance.

    • 360 degree feedback about students to all studetns and their parents.

    • Regular assignments, presentations, project work, assessment, and progress report.

    • We also help fresh graduates and B.Tech passed-out students who are struggling to make carrer in IT.
